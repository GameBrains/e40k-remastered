= CC3. Make the combat resolution roll

.Combat resolution roll
Both players: Roll a D6 and add your close combat bonuses to the result.

* The player with the highest total score wins the combat.
* If the total scores are the same then the outcome hangs in the balance!
Place one Blast marker on every detachment in the combat, and then proceed again from xref:work-out-close-combat-bonuses.adoc[].
